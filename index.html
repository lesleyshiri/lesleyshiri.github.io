About Me

meets business analytics

I'm a Business and Finance Analyst with 8+ years of accounting and finance experience across operations, payroll, accounting, and revenue analysis. I focus on turning complex numbers into clear reporting, practical insights, and measurable business decisions.

My academic background includes a Master of Commerce in Accounting and a Bachelor of Commerce Honours Degree in Accounting, both from Midlands State University, Zimbabwe, plus a National Diploma in Accounting from Masvingo Polytechnic, Zimbabwe.

I am currently an MBA in Business Analytics student at Clarkson University, New York, combining strong accounting fundamentals with modern analytics to improve efficiency, support leadership decisions, and strengthen financial outcomes.
